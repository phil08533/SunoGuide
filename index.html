Free Video Creation

Create professional promo videos for your music without any editing skills.

TikTok Creative Studio (Free)

TikTok offers a free AI-powered video creation tool through their ads platform. You don't need to run any ads — just create a free account.

  1. Create a free TikTok Ads account at the link below
  2. Go to Creative Studio → Image/Text to Video
  3. Upload an image or enter a text prompt describing your video
  4. The AI generates a professional-looking video for free
  5. Download the video and add your song as background audio
  6. Post to TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, YouTube Shorts, or Reddit
Open TikTok Creative Studio →

Video Ideas for Your Songs

  • Lyric videos: Text overlay with your lyrics scrolling over a moody background
  • POV clips: Generate a scene that matches your song's emotion (rainy window, empty road, sunset)
  • Album art animation: Use your cover art as the base image and let AI animate it
  • Story clips: "POV: you finally let go" with cinematic visuals

Tip: The first 3 seconds determine if someone keeps watching. Start with the most emotional visual + your song's hook.

Where to Post

  • TikTok — Use your own sound (distributed via SoundOn). This is your #1 channel.
  • Instagram Reels — Same content, different audience. Older demographics, more likely to stream on Spotify.
  • Facebook Reels — Repurpose the same videos. Low effort, additional reach.
  • YouTube Shorts — YouTube Music streams count as royalties. Every Short is free promotion.
  • Reddit — Post in niche subreddits. Engage genuinely, don't spam.

SoundOn Promo Page Setup

Your promo page is how listeners find you on their preferred streaming platform. This is your main conversion tool.

Setting Up Your Artist Profile

  1. Go to your SoundOn dashboard and navigate to Artist Profile
  2. Add your artist bio — keep it short and match your genre's vibe
  3. Upload a profile photo (can be AI-generated via Canva or Midjourney)
  4. Add social media links (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
  5. Choose which songs to feature as previews on your page
  6. Copy your promo page link — this is your golden URL

Using Your Promo Link

Your SoundOn promo link lets people choose their preferred streaming platform (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.) to listen. Put this link everywhere:

  • TikTok bio (most important)
  • Instagram bio and stories
  • YouTube channel description
  • Reddit posts when sharing music
  • Any social media where you promote

Every piece of content you post should drive people back to this link.

Setting Up Your Sound on TikTok

  1. Upload your music through SoundOn — it gets distributed to TikTok automatically
  2. Your song becomes available as a "sound" on TikTok within 1-2 weeks
  3. Go to your TikTok profile and claim your artist page
  4. Your sounds will be linked to your profile
  5. When you post videos, always use YOUR OWN sound as the audio
  6. When others use your sound, it drives streams and royalties

Pro tip: In every TikTok caption, write "use this sound" — it encourages others to create videos with your music.
